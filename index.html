Our Specials

Take your pick from a selection of our finest dishes

Rolex to Remember

A roadside classic with a twist – omelette, grilled beef/ Chicken/ Sausage and cabbage salad rolled in Ugandan chapatti, along with an assortment of vegetables
Served with home fries, potato wedges or Masala Fries

Lemon pepper Chicken Skewers

Chicken breasts or thighs, seasoned with a combination of lemon zest and black pepper,
with additional ingredients like garlic, salt, and a touch of sweetness resulting in a flavorful, slightly tangy and peppery dish with a distinct citrusy aroma.

Hibachi style Tilapia

Fresh Tilapia marinated in a teriyaki or ponzu sauce and grilled to perfection, resulting in a slightly charred exterior and a juicy interior, with a smoky flavor from the hot griddle.
Typically served with fried rice and vegetables.

Tikka Tikka Time

Grilled, marinated chicken simmered in a rich, creamy, tomato-based sauce infused with aromatic spices

Buldak Noodles

"Spicy Korean Fire Chicken Noodles"(Buldak Bokkeum Myun)
Tossed in a fiery, caramelized gochujang glaze, with tender pulled chicken, hand-cut scallions, and toasted sesame. A bold harmony of sweet, smoky, and searing heat
Inspired by Seoul’s vibrant street food scene, reimagined with artisanal noodles and free-range poultry.

Optional: Served with a chilled cucumber-melon salad to temper the flames.
OR
Ask for ‘Chef’s Fire’ to turn up the spice.

Chicken Ceasar Salad

Also spelled Cesar, César and Cesare
A green salad of romaine lettuce, grilled chicken and croutons dressed with lemon juice (or lime juice), olive oil, egg, Worcestershire sauce, anchovies, garlic, Dijon mustard, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
